What is a paragraph? A paragraph is a series of related sentences
developing a central idea, called the topic. Try to think about
paragraphs in terms of thematic unity: a paragraph is a sentence
or a group of sentences that supports one central, unified idea.
Paragraphs add one idea at a time to your broader argument.
